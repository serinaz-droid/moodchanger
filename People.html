MoodChanger brings personalized AI support to people managing chronic and everyday health challenges, putting clarity and control back in your hands.

01

Allergies

MoodChanger tracks environmental triggers, symptoms, and your body's reactions in real time — helping you predict, avoid, and manage allergy flare-ups before they disrupt your day.

  • Symptom pattern recognition over time
  • Personalized avoidance recommendations
02 Speech support
02

Stuttering

Using biofeedback and stress monitoring, MoodChanger helps individuals who stutter identify emotional and physiological triggers, track progress, and build confidence through guided techniques.

  • Stress & anxiety trigger identification
  • Breathing & relaxation technique guidance
  • Speech confidence progress tracking
